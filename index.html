How to Play

  • Two players take turns
  • On your turn, click balls from one rod to select them
  • You must select at least 1 ball
  • Click Confirm to move selected balls from Side A → Side B
  • The player who leaves a single ball on Side A for the opponent wins!

Quick Tips

  • Range select: Click ball 1, then ball 3 → selects all between
  • Select all: Double-click any ball on a rod
  • Keyboard: Enter = confirm, Escape = undo
